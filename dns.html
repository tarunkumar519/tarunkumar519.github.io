Private DNS on Android

1. Go to Settings → Network & internet → Advanced → Private DNS.

2. Select the Private DNS provider hostname option.

3. Enter dns.tarunx.me/dns-query and hit Save.

Private DNS on iPhones & iPads

1. Download the configuration profile.

2. Open the Settings app.

3. Tap Profile Downloaded.

4. Tap Install in the upper-right corner, and follow the onscreen instructions.
